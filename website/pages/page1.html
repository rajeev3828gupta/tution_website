About Us

Welcome to Guru Gyan Home Tuition, where learning meets excellence! Established in 2012, we are dedicated to providing personalized and high-quality education through our experienced team of over 4,500 professional tutors.

Over the years, we have built a robust network, serving more than 35,000 students and earning the trust of over 15,000 parents. Our focus on quality education has led us to offer a diverse range of services, from school tuitions for classes I to XII, entrance exam coaching for management and medical exams, to specialized coaching for various subjects and boards.

At Guru Gyan Home Tuition, we believe in the power of personalized learning. Our tutors tailor their teaching methods to suit the individual needs of each student, ensuring that they receive the attention and guidance necessary to excel.

Our commitment to education goes beyond academics. We strive to foster a love for learning and critical thinking in our students, preparing them for future success in all aspects of life. With our comprehensive support system, students not only improve their grades but also build confidence and develop essential life skills.
